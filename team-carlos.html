Carlos Amador

Accountant

Carlos serves as an Accountant at Meridian Holdings Group, bringing more than 20 years of experience across Accounting, Controlling, Finance, and Administrative functions. He is known for delivering accurate and timely reporting to C-level leadership and key organizational stakeholders.

Throughout his career, Carlos has developed deep expertise across multiple Finance and Accounting disciplines, including AR/AP, Treasury, Cost Accounting, Operations, HR, Logistics, and Sales support. His broad operational background enables him to effectively manage financial processes and drive profitable results within complex organizational environments.

Carlos has a strong track record of implementing policies, procedures, and best-practice frameworks across company departments. He has built and delivered KPI dashboards, Balanced Scorecards, and presentations that equip Directors with the information necessary for effective decision-making. His experience also includes the creation of organizational manuals, integration of internal controls, and resource optimization initiatives.

With significant experience in ERP implementation and other system tools, Carlos has repeatedly led organizations through process improvements and structural enhancements. His skill set spans leadership, analysis, organization, negotiation, team building, bi-directional communication, and executive-level reporting. He has also delivered professional presentations in the U.S., Mexico, Paris, Germany, and Brazil.

Highly professional, proactive, and hands-on, Carlos is recognized for his commitment to excellence, adaptability, and ethical approach to teamwork and organizational management.
